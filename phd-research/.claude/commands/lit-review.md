Conduct a full literature review with ZERO questions: $ARGUMENTS

1. Generate 5-8 keyword combinations from different angles
2. Search arXiv, Google Scholar, Semantic Scholar
3. Collect 20+ papers: foundational, methodological, competitors, recent
4. Classify each by: type, citation count, venue, relevance
5. Synthesize into a research landscape summary
6. Identify gaps, positioning opportunities, and strongest baselines
7. Generate BibTeX for all papers
8. Write to docs/literature/

If topic is broad, cover ALL subfields. Never ask which papers to include.
