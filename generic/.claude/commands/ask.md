Answer this research question using the knowledge base, ZERO questions asked: $ARGUMENTS

## Protocol

1. **Search the wiki first**: Read `wiki/indexes/_index.md` and `wiki/indexes/_concepts.md` to find relevant articles
2. **Read relevant articles**: Navigate via backlinks and connections to gather all related context
3. **Synthesize**: Combine wiki knowledge to answer the question. If the wiki doesn't have enough, supplement with your own knowledge and flag what's from wiki vs. general knowledge.
4. **File back**: Save the answer as a new article in `wiki/outputs/articles/` with full frontmatter, connections, and concept links
5. **Update indexes**: Add the new article to all relevant indexes
6. **Add backlinks**: Update existing articles that are now connected to this answer

## Answer Format

The answer article should include:
- Summary (direct answer, 2-3 sentences)
- Detailed analysis (with references to wiki articles as `[[wikilinks]]`)
- Evidence (from wiki sources and/or general knowledge, clearly distinguished)
- Open questions (what this answer doesn't resolve)
- Connections to existing wiki articles

## Key Rule
The answer becomes part of the wiki. Future questions can build on this answer. Every exploration compounds.
